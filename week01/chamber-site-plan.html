Our Mission

Our community's online hangout spot! This website is where we share what's going on, offer handy services, and connect with each other. Whether it's discovering local events, finding local businesses, or just chatting with neighbors, this is the virtual hub for all of us!

Color Schema

The main color will be #135784 a beautiful blue, its use will be in the headings and navigation bar.

The secondary color will be #964b00. This is an awesome brown and will be used in accents and selectors.

Typograph

The fonts will be Montserrat and Roboto. Montserrat will be the headings and Roboto the body.
